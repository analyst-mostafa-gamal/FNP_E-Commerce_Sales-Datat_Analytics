## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a data-driven analysis of 1,000 e-commerce transactions for **FNP**, an international gifting platform. The goal was to identify seasonal trends, high-value product categories, and peak operational windows to optimize marketing efforts and inventory management.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit: The "Advanced Excel" Stack
Unlike standard spreadsheet reporting, this project utilizes the full Excel Power Suite to mimic a professional BI environment:

**Power Query (ETL):** Automated the extraction, cleaning, and merging of three relational tables (Orders, Products, Customers).

**Power Pivot (Data Modeling):** Established a relational data model (Star Schema logic) to link customer demographics with product performance.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Engineered custom measures to calculate complex KPIs such as **Average Order Value (AOV)**.

Pivot Tables & Interactive Dashboards: Built a dynamic reporting interface using slicers and timelines for real-time data exploration.

## 📈 Key Business Insights & KPIs
**Total Revenue:** $3,520,984 

**Total Orders:** 1,000

**Average Order Value (AOV):** $3,521

**Operational Efficiency:** Average delivery time is 5.5 days.

**1. Seasonal Demand & Revenue Surges**
- **The Q1 Peak:** Identified a massive revenue surge in **February ($704k)** and **March ($511k)**, driven by Valentine's Day and Holi.

- **Seasonality Gap:** Revenue in February is approximately **500% higher** than in April, indicating a sharp drop-off as the business moves into the second quarter.

**2. High-Growth Categories & Occasions**
- **Top Categories:** **"Colors"** ($1,005,645) and **"Soft Toys"** ($740,831) are the primary revenue drivers.

- **Occasion Logic:** **"Anniversary"** and **"Raksha Bandhan"** represent the most consistent high-value gifting triggers.

**3. Customer & Hourly Trends**
- **Peak Transaction Window:** Data shows that the highest volume of orders occurs between **7:00 PM** and **8:00 PM**.

- **Demographics:** Market share is split nearly equally between Male (51%) and Female (49%) customers.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the analytical findings, the following business actions are recommended:

**Marketing Optimization:** Trigger digital ad campaigns and email marketing at **6:30 PM** to align with the identified 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM peak buying window.

**Budget Allocation:** Reallocate 25% of the marketing budget from low-performing months (January/April) to high-impact seasonal windows in **February/March**.

**Inventory Leveling:** Increase safety stock for the "Colors" and "Soft Toys" categories at least 10 days prior to the **Anniversary** and **Holi** peaks to ensure 100% fulfillment.
